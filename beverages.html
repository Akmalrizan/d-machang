Chatea

Chatea serve a very premium cold beverages from tea based, fruit based, chocolate based, and coffee based. The shop was mainly inspired by tea. That is why it is name as ChaTea. 'Cha' in chinese means tea.

The operating hour for Chatea is from 10am until 10pm.

Address: Farmasi Lautan Machang, Lot 295A Bandar Lama, 18500 Machang, Kelantan


Kopi Kita

The operating hour of Kopi Kita station is from 11am to 7pm. It is open for everyday.

Address: Kopi Kita 31 Shell Batu 31,Batu 31, 18500 Machang, Kelantan

Bingsu Poksu

Bingsu Poksu is a famous beverage stall of Machang town. The cool drink was adapted from Korean famous beverage and it is in trend for teenagers. They love the combination of sour fruit and sweet milk that come together from this ice dessert.

Bingsu Poksu operate from 10am until 5pm everyday.

Address: Jalan Bakat, Pekan Machang, 18500 Machang, Kelantan
